We've made some improvements to the site that make it easier to find recipes, build weekly meal plans, and plan a grocery list. We hope you like the new look and find the site easier to use than ever.

How to Use the Diabetes Food Hub

  1. Create an Account: First things first—if you have not done so already, create your free account with Diabetes Food Hub. Not only will creating an account let you save recipes, create a profile, and enjoy a more personalized experience on the site, but access to the Meal Planner and Grocery List features requires an account.
  2. Browse Recipes: Based on feedback we've received from users, we've made it easier than ever to browse and find the recipes you're looking for. The homepage of Diabetes Food Hub will automatically show some of the most popular recipes on the site.
  3. Save Recipes: Once you've found recipes you like, be sure to save them to the My Recipes section, which you can view by clicking the My Recipes section of the navigation bar.
  4. Start Building: Now that you’ve saved some recipes, it’s time to start building! Your saved recipes will show up to the right of the interactive Meal Planner.
  5. Create a Grocery List: Once you’ve built out your meal plan, you can make shopping a snap by clicking on the “Generate Grocery List” button at the top of the meal planner.
